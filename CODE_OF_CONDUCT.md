# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism

Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:
- The use of sexual language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the repository maintainers.

For full details, see: https://www.contributor-covenant.org
